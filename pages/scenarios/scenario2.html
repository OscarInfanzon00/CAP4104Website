Sarah's Journey to Securing an Internship

1. Login to OnlineStudyHub

Sarah logs in and navigates to the internship resources section.

2. Access Internship Resources

Sarah explores articles and videos on how to find and apply for internships.

3. Learn How to Craft a Strong Resume

Sarah uses templates and tips provided on the platform to create a standout resume.

4. Understand What to Look for in an Internship

Sarah learns about key factors to consider, such as company culture, mentorship opportunities, and job responsibilities.

5. Utilize Interview Preparation Resources

Sarah practices common interview questions and learns effective interview techniques.

6. Connect with Tutors for Real-Time Assistance

Sarah schedules sessions with tutors to get personalized advice on her application process.

7. Participate in Mock Interviews

Sarah engages in mock interviews with peers or tutors to build confidence.

8. Outcome

Sarah feels well-prepared and confident as she applies for internships and attends interviews.
