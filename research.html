Research on Online Tutoring

This page provides an overview of research evidence for the creation and deployment of our online tutoring service.

1. The Effectiveness of Online Learning

Studies show that at least online learning has the same quality of traditional classroom learning if proper pedagogy skills are used. It proves that online programs with structure improves learner performance.

3. Best Practices for Online Tutoring

Some of the best practices includes: Communication policies, setting learning outcomes, and using teaching aids. They are useful in developing the right climate for learning in the online environment.

4. Accessibility in Online Education

It is crucial to make online classes accessible to all students, as we face this new learning model. Implementing the guideline of the universal design improves learning accessibility and utilization for different learners.

5. Future Trends in Online Tutoring

The continual advancement of technologies is likely to exhibit popularity of application of artificial intelligence and individualized learning approaches. Teaching methodologies that are student centered ensure that the latters gets what he or she wants because of the diversity in society.
